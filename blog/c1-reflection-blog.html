Reflections on EDA Bootcamp style learning

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

My point of view is skewed in that I already have a tech background. But I can say this.
Don't be scared, I know it sounds hardcore and you probably thought you'd never end up in a Bootcamp for programming, when you were in school.
let me tell you now, it is better that you can see the way the world is heading and that you are doing something about it now, than if you left it another 5 years.
Yes, you're a bit scared, but on the other side of fear is a state of elation and possibility that you can't fathom right now. All you need to do is keep putting one foot after the other
